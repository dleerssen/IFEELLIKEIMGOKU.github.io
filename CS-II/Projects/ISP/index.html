Back End Model Scheduler

For my Computer Science II Independent Study Project (ISP), I will be helping with creating the Allen High School Model Scheduler. The Model Scheduler helps students make class schedules for next school year. The program tells the students which classes are available for what periods, and if the students' schedules would work for next school year. The Back End of the Model Scheduler, which is what my group will be working on, is the data driven portion of the Model Scheduler. Basically, we will be gathering data about all the classes, such as when they're available, where they are, and whether they are one semester or two.
